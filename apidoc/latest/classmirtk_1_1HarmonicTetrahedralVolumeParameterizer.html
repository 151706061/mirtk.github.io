Detailed Description

Discrete volumetric harmonic map

This implementation is based on

Paillé & Poulin (2012), As-conformal-as-possible discrete volumetric mapping, Computers and Graphics (Pergamon), 36(5), 427–433. doi:10.1016/j.cag.2012.03.014

The discrete volumetric harmonic map was first presented in

Wang et al. (2004), Volumetric harmonic map, Communications in Information and Systems, 3(3), 191–202.
