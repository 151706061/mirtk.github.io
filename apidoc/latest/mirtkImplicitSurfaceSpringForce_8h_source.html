32 /**
33  * Force used to smooth sharp edges during shrink wrapping an implicit surface
34  *
35  * This force vanishes nearby the target isosurface defined by the discretized
36  * implicit surface distance function. Further away from the isosurface, it
37  * is equivalent to the QuadraticCurvatureConstraint force which aims
38  * to minimize the average distance of neighboring nodes to the tangent plane
39  * by moving the tangent plane in normal direction.
40  */
